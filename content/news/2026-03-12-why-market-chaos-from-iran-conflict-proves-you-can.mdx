## What This Means for Your Retirement

If you're 55 or older with most of your retirement savings in a traditional 401(k) or IRA, this market chaos should be a wake-up call. **Your retirement security shouldn't depend on whether you can successfully predict the outcome of international conflicts.**

Think about it: Do you really want to bet your golden years on guessing whether Iran tensions will escalate or de-escalate next month? Whether oil will hit $100 or crash to $60?

**This is why savers are losers in today's system.** The financial establishment has trained Americans to put their retirement money into paper assets that swing wildly based on news cycles, Fed announcements, and geopolitical events completely outside your control.
