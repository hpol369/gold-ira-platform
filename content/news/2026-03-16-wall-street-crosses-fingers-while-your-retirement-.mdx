## What This Means for Your Retirement

If your 401(k) or IRA is heavily weighted in stocks, you're essentially betting your retirement on global stability. **Every time there's a crisis in the Middle East, your nest egg swings up or down.**

Let's say you have $500,000 in your retirement account, with 80% in stock funds. A 10% market drop due to supply chain disruptions wipes out $40,000 of your savings overnight. And you have zero control over it.

**This is the new reality: Your retirement is hostage to events happening thousands of miles away.** Oil tankers, shipping lanes, foreign conflicts - none of this should determine whether you can retire comfortably. But in today's interconnected, fragile financial system, it does.
