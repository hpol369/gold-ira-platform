## What This Means for Your Retirement

If you're 55+ and watching crypto swings affect your retirement accounts, you're learning a hard lesson about volatility risk. **Your retirement timeline can't handle 20% daily swings** - you need that money to be there when you need it.

Here's the math that matters: If you're planning to retire in 5-10 years, you can't afford to have your nest egg disappear during a crypto winter. We've seen Bitcoin lose 80% of its value before. Imagine that happening to your IRA right when you need to start taking distributions.

This doesn't mean crypto is evil or worthless. It means **diversification matters more than ever.** The dollar is still being devalued through money printing. Inflation is still eating your savings. But betting your retirement on any single volatile asset - whether it's crypto, stocks, or anything else - is financial suicide.
