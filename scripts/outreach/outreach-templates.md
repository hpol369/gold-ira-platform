## Notes on Outreach Best Practices

1. **Personalize every email** — reference specific content on their site
2. **Lead with value** — what's in it for them, not you
3. **One ask per email** — don't bundle requests
4. **Follow up once** — after 5-7 days, max 2 total contacts
5. **Track everything** — log in outreach-tracker.json
6. **Never mention backlinks** — frame it as "resource," "citation," "reference"
7. **Send Tuesday-Thursday 9-11am** in recipient's timezone
8. **Keep it under 150 words** — nobody reads long pitches
