## Conversion Tracking Setup

**Conversion Action:** "Confirmed Call Request"
- **Trigger:** gtag fires only when user clicks "Yes, Call Me" in the modal confirmation step
- **Value:** $50 (will update to $200 once qualified lead value confirmed)
- **Count:** One per click
- **Window:** 30 days

**Important:** The conversion pixel does NOT fire on initial form submission — only on the confirmation step "Yes, Call Me" button. This means Google Ads optimizes for users who actually want a call, leading to higher pickup rates at Augusta.

---

## Notes
- Start all 3 ad groups simultaneously at €50/day each
- After 2 weeks (or 30 conversions), enable tCPA bidding
- Monitor Quality Score — aim for 7+ on all keywords
- Pause low-performing keywords after 100 impressions with 0 conversions
- Scale winner ad group first (increase budget by 20%/week)
